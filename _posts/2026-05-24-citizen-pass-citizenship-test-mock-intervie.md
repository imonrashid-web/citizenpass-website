## Why Use Mock Interviews?

Most study tools force you to look at flashcards or study individual sections in isolation. But on your actual interview day, everything happens at once.

The Citizen Pass Mock Interview module replicates the true flow of the official USCIS interview. It packages all four crucial components into a single, cohesive run-through:

1. The N-400 Questions: Personal history, employment, travel, and moral character.
2. Reading Test: Reading official sentences aloud.
3. Writing Test: Writing sentences from dictation.
4. Civics Test: Randomly selected questions where you need 6 out of 10 correct to pass.

By practicing the entire sequence together, nothing will feel new or surprising when you step into the room for your real appointment.
