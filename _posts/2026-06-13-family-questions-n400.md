How to Answer Questions  About Your Family in the N-400 Interview 🇺🇸

About your spouse:

❓ Are you married?

❓ Spouse's full name?

❓ Is your spouse a US  citizen?

❓ When and where did you  marry?

About your children:

❓ Do you have children?

❓ Their names and dates of  birth?

Tips:

✅ Know your spouse's full  name, DOB, and citizenship status

✅ Know your marriage date  and location

✅ Know all children's full  names and dates of birth

The officer just needs facts  — they are not judging your family situation!
