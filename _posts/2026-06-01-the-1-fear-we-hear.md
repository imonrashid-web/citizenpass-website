The #1 fear we hear: “What if I don’t understand the officer?”



Good news: you have options. Better news: officers expect this.

If you don’t understand, here’s what to do:

✅ *“Could you please repeat the question?”* — totally fine, ask as many times as you need ✅ *“Could you say that more slowly, please?”* — also fine ✅ *“Could you rephrase that?”* — also fine

What not to do:

❌ Pretend you understood and guess ❌ Stay silent ❌ Answera different question

Officers will repeat. They will slow down.They will rephrase. They want you to understand the question — becausethey need to know your answer is accurate.

The English requirement is *“ordinary English.”* Notperfect English. Not legal English. Ordinary English — the kind you’d use totalk to a neighbor.

But here’s the catch: you need to practice these recoveryphrases out loud. Reading them isn’t enough. Under stress, even simpleEnglish vanishes if you haven’t rehearsed it.
