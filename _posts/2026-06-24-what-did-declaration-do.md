What did the Declaration of Independence do? 🇺🇸

Understanding how the United States broke away from Great Britain is a cornerstone of the 2025 Civics test.

An insider secret tip for this question: You have three acceptable ways to state this function. You can say it announced our independence, declared our independence, or said that the United States is free from Great Britain. Choosing the exact phrase "announced our independence" is clean and easy to memorize.

Practical example scenario: If you overexplain by listing historical figures or dates instead of stating what the document physically did, the officer will have to ask you again.
