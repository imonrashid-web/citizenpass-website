Geographical terms make up a massive portion of the sentencesyou will be asked to read during the USCIS English test. If you can confidentlyread state names and major locations, you are halfway to passing.

Make sure you can read these Places & Geography words effortlessly:
📍 United States, U.S., America
📍 Washington, D.C., White House, Capital
📍 Alaska, California, New York, Texas
📍 Canada, Mexico

Note that "Washington" can refer to the state, the capital city, orthe first President depending on the context of the sentence. Practicingsentences containing these terms out loud is crucial to avoid gettingtongue-tied.
