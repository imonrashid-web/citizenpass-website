Tips for Answering N-400 Questions Truthfully

The most important rule: TELL THE TRUTH.

Practical tips:
✅ If you don't understand, say "Can you repeat that?" — never guess
✅ If unsure about a date, say "approximately"
✅ If the answer is "yes" — say yes. USCIS checks records
✅ Don't volunteer extra information — answer and stop
✅ If your N-400 has an error, bring a correction letter

Your job: confirm what is on your application — don't change it!
