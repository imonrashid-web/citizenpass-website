The officer will ask questions directly from your application. Main categories:

📋 Name, address, contact information
📋 Travel history — trips outside the US
📋 Marital history — married, divorced, widowed?
📋 Children — names and dates of birth
📋 Employment history
📋 Organizations and affiliations
📋 Good moral character questions

The key: Tell the truth and be consistent with your application. The officer already has your form!
