## What You'll Find in Citizen Pass

**Official USCIS Civics Questions (2008 & 2025 Sets)**  
All 228 official civics questions with flashcards, practice tests, and progress tracking. The app includes both the 2008 (100 questions) and 2025 (128 questions) test versions, so you're studying the right material for your interview date.

**Reading & Writing Practice — Just Like the Real Test**  
50 reading sentences and 50 writing sentences from the official USCIS vocabulary list. Practice recognizing and spelling key civics and everyday vocabulary words exactly as they appear on the test.

**N-400 Personal Interview Questions**  
Review the questions the USCIS officer will ask about your N-400 application. Practice answering clearly and confidently in a realistic format.

**Full Mock Interview, Start to Finish**  
Simulate the complete citizenship interview experience. The mock interview covers all sections in the order you'll encounter them at USCIS, and you get scored on every answer so you know where you stand.

**Answers Tailored to Your State**  
Some civics questions depend on where you live — your governor, senators, representative, and state capital. Enter your ZIP code once, and Citizen Pass automatically shows you the correct answers for your location.
