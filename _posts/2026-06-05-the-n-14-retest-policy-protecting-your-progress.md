The naturalization interview framework has a built-in safety net to protect your efforts:
✅ Your Passes are Locked In: If you pass your 100 Civics questions and the Reading portion but stumble three times on the Writing portion, your successful Civics and Reading scores are saved permanently in the system. You do not start over from scratch!
✅ The Form N-14 Safety Net: The officer will hand you a Form N-14 and automatically schedule a follow-up retest appointment (typically within 60 to 90 days).
✅ Targeted Retesting: At your second appointment, you will be tested ONLY on the specific sub-section you missed—which means you'll just write sentences.
