Membership & Affiliations Questions in Your N-400 Interview

Part 7 asks about organizations you've belonged to in the last 10 years:

Include:
📋 Professional associations
📋 Religious organizations
📋 Community groups

Must disclose:
⚠️ Political parties (home country)
⚠️ Any government-designated terrorist organization

Tips:
✅ Be honest — list all organizations you remember
✅ Don't hide political party memberships
✅ When in doubt, include it
