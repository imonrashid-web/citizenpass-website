Heads up: there are TWO versions of the civics test right now.

✅ 2008 version — 100 questions. The officer asks up to 10. You need6 to pass.

✅ 2025 version — 128 questions. Same passing rule (6 out of 10).

The 2025 test is being phased in for newer applicants. If you filedbefore the transition date, you'll likely take the 2008 version. After, the2025 version.

The questions overlap a lot — but the 2025 version has 28 additionalquestions covering newer topics in American government, history, and geography.

💡 Pro tip: Study the version thatmatches your filing date. Don't waste time memorizing the wrong question set.

The Citizen Pass app lets you toggle between both versions so youcan study the right one — or both, if you're not sure.
