Travel History — What to Expect in Your N-400 Interview 🇺🇸

Travel history is one of the most important parts of the N-400 interview.

Be ready for:
❓ How many trips did you take outside the US?
❓ Where did you go?
❓ How long were you outside each time?
❓ Was any trip longer than 6 months?

Key rules:
⚠️ Trips over 6 months can affect your continuous residence requirement
⚠️ Trips over 1 year are very serious

Tips:
✅ Have a list of trips ready — dates, destinations, length
✅ Be honest — USCIS can check passport records
