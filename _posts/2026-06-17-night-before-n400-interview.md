How to Prepare the Night Before Your N-400 Interview 🇺🇸

Documents:
✅ Green card
✅ Photo ID
✅ USCIS appointment notice
✅ Copy of your N-400
✅ Tax returns (just in case)
✅ Marriage/divorce certificates if applicable

Tonight (30 min max):
✅ Read your N-400 one time
✅ Review difficult civics answers
✅ Practice saying your address and employment clearly

Then:
✅ Set your alarm with extra time
✅ Know the USCIS address
✅ Get a good night's sleep! 😴
