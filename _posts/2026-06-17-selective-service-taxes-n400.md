Selective Service & Tax Questions in Your N-400

SELECTIVE SERVICE:
- Males who lived in US ages 18-26 must register
- If yes: ✅ Simply say "Yes"
- If female or arrived after 26: "This does not apply to me"
- Did not register when required? Get a Status Information Letter

TAXES:
- If you filed: ✅ Confirm and you're done
- Did not file? File missing returns BEFORE your interview!
- Income below threshold? Say "I was not required to file"
