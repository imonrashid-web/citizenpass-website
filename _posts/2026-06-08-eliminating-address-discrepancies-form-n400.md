 One of the most common  friction points during the active N-400 interview is your address history  timeline. The processing officer will cross-verify your written application  lines against official residential data to check for gaps or overlapping  dates.
  
  Before stepping inside the center, review every location you have lived at  over the past five years. Making sure your rental leases, tax returns, and  application addresses match perfectly protects you from intense  cross-examination or unexpected application delays.
