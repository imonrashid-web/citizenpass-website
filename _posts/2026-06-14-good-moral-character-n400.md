What Does 'Good Moral Character' Mean for Your N-400?

USCIS requires "good moral character" for the past 5 years:

✅ No serious criminal convictions
✅ Filed taxes every year
✅ Registered for Selective Service (males 18-26 in US)

Minor traffic violations do NOT automatically disqualify you.

Can disqualify:
⚠️ Felony convictions
⚠️ Lying to USCIS
⚠️ Multiple DUIs
⚠️ Failure to pay taxes

Please use the following link for official USCIS information about Good Moral Character: 
https://www.uscis.gov/policy-manual/volume-12-part-f-chapter-4
