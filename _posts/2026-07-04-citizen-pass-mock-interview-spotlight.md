	Flashcards can teach you facts, but they can't teach you what interview day feels like. Citizen Pass's Mock Interview mode simulates the real USCIS appointment — Civics questions asked the way an officer asks them, Reading and Writing prompts in sequence, and N-400 review questions, all in one realistic run-through. Walk into your actual interview having already practiced the real format, not just memorized answers.
