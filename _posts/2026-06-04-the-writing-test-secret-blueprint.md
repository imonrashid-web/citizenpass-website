**Did you know** that the N-400 Reading Test and the Writing Test are directly connected? Understanding this hidden link can immediately reduce your stress on interview day. Here is the insider secret:
The sentence the officer asks you to READ is almost always a question.
The sentence the officer asks you to WRITE is the direct answer to that question!


**For example:**
If your reading sentence is: "Who was the first President?"
Your writing sentence will likely be: "Washington was the first President."
By paying close attention to the question you read, you get a massive hint about the words you will have to spell and write next. It gives you a moment to mentally prepare your spelling!
