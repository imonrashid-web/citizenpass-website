How to Answer Questions About Your Address 🇺🇸

The officer will ask about your address early in the interview:

❓ What is your current address?
❓ How long have you lived there?
❓ What was your previous address?

Tips:
✅ Know your address by heart — number, street, city, state, ZIP
✅ Know approximately when you moved to your current address
✅ Be honest about any time outside the US

The officer already has your N-400 — they are just confirming your information is accurate!
