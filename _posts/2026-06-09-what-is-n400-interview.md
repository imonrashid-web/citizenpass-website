The N-400 interview is themost important step in your naturalization journey.

Here is what happens:

✅ The officer reviews your N-400 application line by line

✅ You answer questions aboutyour personal history, family, and background

✅ You take the English Readingand Writing test

✅ You answer up to 20 Civicsquestions (2025 test) or 10 questions (2008 test)

 The interview usually takes20-30 minutes. The officer is NOT there to trick you — they want you tosucceed!
