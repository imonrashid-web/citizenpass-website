### The #1 mistake people make studying for the civics test? Studying silently.

Reading the 100 (or 128) questions and answers over and over feels productive. But on test day, you're not asked to recognize the answer — you're asked to say it. Out loud. In English. To a real person.

Those are completely different skills.

Try this instead: 🗣️ Read each question out loud 🗣️ Say your answer out loud — even if no one's listening 🗣️ If you stumble, repeat it 3x until it flows naturally 🗣️ Bonus: record yourself and listen back

Your tongue needs the practice as much as your brain does.
