The English Reading Test  component of your N-400 naturalization interview is highly structured and  entirely predictable. The USCIS officer will show you a sentence on a digital  tablet screen or a physical piece of paper and ask you to read it out loud.
  
  To pass, you must read exactly ONE out of THREE sentences correctly. The  vocabulary words are drawn strictly from the official civic public list,  containing simple historical names, locations, and legislative titles. There  are no trick readings or advanced literary paragraphs.
