Marital Status Questions in Your N-400 Interview

Married: ✅ Spouse's full name, DOB, citizenship status + marriage date and location

Divorced: ✅ Date divorce was finalized, former spouse's name, which court

Widowed: ✅ Former spouse's name and date of death

Never married: ✅ Simply say "I have never been married"

The officer checks your answers match your N-400 form — be consistent!
