The officer will ask aboutyour work history for the last 5 years:

 

❓ Are you currently employed?

❓ Where do you work?

❓ What is your job title?

❓ How long have you workedthere?

❓ Where did you work before?

 

Tips:

✅ Know your employer name,address, and how long you've worked there

✅ If self-employed, know yourbusiness name and type

✅ If unemployed, say soclearly — it is NOT disqualifying
