The N-400 English Writing Test  measures your fundamental ability to write basic sentences cleanly in English  as an officer speaks. The processing agent will read an official sentence out  loud up to three times, and you must write it onto a tablet device.
  
  To secure a pass, you only need to write ONE out of THREE sentences  correctly. Spacing, general legibility, and capturing core content words are  what the officer marks. You do not need perfect creative prose; you just need  to capture the exact structure dictated from the official word list.
