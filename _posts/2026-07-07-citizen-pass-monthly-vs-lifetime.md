Not sure which plan makes sense for you? Monthly is $2.99/mo, flexible and cancel anytime — great if your interview is coming up soon and you just need focused prep. Lifetime is $9.99 one-time, less than the cost of one dinner out, and unlocks everything forever — great if you want to keep coming back to review or help a family member prepare too. Either way, Civics, Reading, and Writing stay free forever; these plans just unlock the full Mock Interview and N-400 experience.
