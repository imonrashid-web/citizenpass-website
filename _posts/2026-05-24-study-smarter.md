The #1 mistake people make studying for the civics test? Studyingsilently.

Reading the 100 (or 128) questions and answers over and over feelsproductive. But on test day, you're not asked to *recognize* the answer —you're asked to *say* it. Out loud. In English. To a real person.

Those are completely different skills.

Try this instead: 🗣️ Read each question out loud 🗣️ Say your answer out loud — even if noone's listening 🗣️ If you stumble, repeat it 3x until itflows naturally 🗣️ Bonus: record yourself and listenback

Your tongue needs the practice as much as your brain does.
