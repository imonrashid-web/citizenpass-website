Everything You Need

Six modules. One goal.

Designed specifically for the USCIS naturalization interview. Study at your own pace with questions that match the real test.

📚

228 Civics Questions

All official USCIS civics questions with flashcards, practice tests, and progress tracking. Includes the 2008 (100 questions) and 2025 (128 questions) test versions.

📖

Reading Practice

50 reading sentences from the official USCIS vocabulary list. Practice recognizing key civics and everyday vocabulary words. Audio pronunciation included.

✏️

Writing Practice

50 writing sentences to practice spelling and grammar. Master the words and sentence patterns used in the writing portion of the interview.

📋

N-400 Preparation

Review the questions the officer will ask about your N-400 application. Practice answering clearly and confidently in a realistic format.

🎤

Mock Interview

Simulate the full citizenship interview experience. Covers all sections in the order you will encounter them at USCIS. Get scored and review every answer.

📍

State-Specific Answers

Enter your ZIP code and get the correct governor, senators, and representative for your location. The real answers you need for your interview.

🌙

Dark Mode & Offline

Study comfortably day or night with full dark mode support. Works completely offline — no internet connection required.
